FAQ - AI Workout Generator

The AI workout generator uses advanced algorithms to analyze your fitness data and preferences, creating personalized workout plans tailored to your goals and capabilities.

Is the AI able to adapt my workout routine over time?

Yes, the AI continuously adapts your workout routine based on your progress, feedback, and any changes in your fitness level or goals.

What types of exercises are included in the workout plans?

The workout plans include a variety of exercises, including cardio, strength training, flexibility exercises, and more. The AI aims to provide a well-rounded and enjoyable fitness experience.
